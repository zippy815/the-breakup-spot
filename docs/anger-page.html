Anger


You have to deal with a lot of different emotions when going through a breakup. Anger is one the most difficult ones to deal with. The key is to recognize when anger can help you, and make your anger work for you, so it will become a healing tool for you during your breakup. Dealing with a breakup ultimately means dealing with your emotions.
